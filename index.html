Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have a Spotify account to use Moody?

Yes, you need an account. Spotify is free to sign up.

What if I don't have Spotify downloaded on my phone?

We reccomend that you do so that you can play full songs instead of listening to the previews only.

Will you ever charge?

Nope! Moody is free and always will be!

Can I save these playlists to my account?

If you have the Spotify app downloaded on your phone you can!
